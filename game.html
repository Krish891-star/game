How to Play

Rock Paper Scissors Rules

Rock Paper Scissors is a simple hand game played between two players. Each player simultaneously forms one of three shapes with an outstretched hand.

The Rules:

✊ Rock crushes ✂️ Scissors

✋ Paper covers ✊ Rock

✂️ Scissors cuts ✋ Paper

In this championship, you'll play 3 rounds with 6 games each in each round. The player with the highest score at the end of each round wins that round. The ultimate champion is determined after all 3 rounds!

Privacy Policy for Ghibli RPS Championship

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how we handle your information in the Ghibli RPS Championship game. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe gaming experience.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information from players. The game operates entirely within your browser and does not transmit any data to external servers.

Game Data

All game progress and scores are stored locally on your device. This data is not shared with anyone and is deleted when you clear your browser cache.
